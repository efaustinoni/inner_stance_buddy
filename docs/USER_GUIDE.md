# Exercise Journal — Beta User Guide

## What is this app?
Exercise Journal is your personal companion to the Inner Stance coaching program. During your sessions you receive exercises and reflection questions each week. This app gives you **one place per quarter** to store them, save or update your answers, add personal notes, and track your daily progress — so nothing gets lost between sessions. 

---

## Your data is private and encrypted
Everything you enter — questions, answers, reflections and progress notes — is **encrypted in the database**. Even the platform administrator cannot read your personal content; they only see encrypted ciphertext. Your data is protected at three levels:

1. **Authentication** — only you can log in to your account (email + password).
2. **Row-Level Security** — the database enforces that each user can only access their own data. It is technically impossible for another user to see your content.
3. **Column Encryption** — your answers, question text, and notes are encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES encryption. The encryption key is stored in a secure vault separate from your data. Password resets do not affect your encrypted data.

> *Note: This is a beta platform. While your content is encrypted, structural data (week numbers, quarter names, topics from the coaching program) remains visible to the administrator for support purposes. Do not store highly confidential business secrets.*

---

## Quarters
Each quarter of the program gets its own label (e.g. *Q2-2026* or *Q2 - Effectiveness*).

- Go to **Manage Weeks → Manage Quarters** to create, rename or delete quarters.
- All your weeks and exercises are grouped under their quarter on the Dashboard.

---

## Adding a week of exercises
Your coach provides the weekly exercises. You can get them into the app in four ways:

| Mode | When to use |
|---|---|
| **From Image** | Take a photo of your printed exercise sheet — AI reads and imports everything automatically |
| **Import CSV** | If you receive a CSV file from your coach |
| **Paste Text** | Copy-paste the exercise text directly |
| **Manual** | Type in questions yourself or add personal reflection points |

Go to **Manage Weeks → Add New Week** and choose a mode.

---

## Manage Weeks
All your weeks are listed here, grouped by the selected quarter filter.

- **Quarter badges** at the top filter the list — click one to show only that quarter's weeks.
- Each **week row** starts collapsed — click it to expand and see its questions.
- The **gear icon** (⚙) on each row opens the edit menu for that week.
- Use **Collapse All** (top right) to collapse all open weeks at once.
- **Add Question** and **Bulk Import** buttons appear inside each expanded week.

---

## Dashboard
Your central overview of all exercises across the program.

- **Filter** by quarter, week, or keyword to find what you need.
- **Click a week header** to collapse or expand its questions.
- **Change the quarter** of a week using the dropdown next to the week title.
- **Click any question** to open it, write or update your answer, or add reflection notes.

---

## Progress tracking
Some exercises are meant to be practiced every day. For those, activate daily tracking:

1. Open the question and click **Track this question**.
2. Each day of the week, check it off as done (or not).
3. Your active tracked exercises appear in the **Progress** section.

This is especially useful for behavioural commitments like *"Did I apply this today?"*

---

## Tips
- Add your own **personal reflection points** as extra questions in any week — the app is flexible. Use it to capture anything you want to remember for your sessions.
- The **quarter filter** keeps your current quarter focused; past quarters stay accessible but out of the way.
- The **From Image** import works best with clear, well-lit photos of printed sheets.
- The version number in the bottom-left corner confirms you're on the latest update.
